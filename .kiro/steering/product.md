# Product: Excel Accounting Services Website

A multi-page marketing website for Excel Accounting Services, a South African accounting firm based in the Western Cape (offices in Fishhoek and Strand/Helderberg).

## Purpose
Modernised rebuild of the existing site — retains all original content while improving design, responsiveness, SEO, and lead generation.

## Pages
- `/` — Home (hero, intro, service cards, pillars, full services list, tax accordion, clients)
- `/about` — Company history and values
- `/services` — Services overview with 6 service cards
- `/services/[slug]` — Individual service subpages (accounting, tax, secretarial, payroll, legal, bookkeeping)
- `/contact` — Contact form + office details

## Key Features
- Floating WhatsApp button on all pages (`https://wa.me/27217828927`)
- Scroll-triggered CTA animations (respects `prefers-reduced-motion`)
- Functional contact form via Resend API
- Per-service enquiry forms pre-filled with service name
- Full SEO: `generateMetadata()`, Open Graph, JSON-LD, sitemap, robots.txt
